The Challenge

Ambience Healthcare is an AI-powered medical scribe startup that automates clinical documentation for healthcare providers. As a consultant, I was brought in to help with:

  • Developing go-to-market strategy for a competitive AI healthcare market
  • Building CRM infrastructure to support rapid growth
  • Creating marketing automation for lead nurturing and qualification
  • Positioning against established medical scribe companies
  • Targeting provider workflows and clinical documentation use cases

The Approach

I focused on establishing marketing technology infrastructure and go-to-market foundations:

Go-to-Market Strategy
  • Developed positioning strategy focusing on AI efficiency gains for clinical workflows
  • Identified target personas: physicians, practice managers, and health system administrators
  • Created messaging framework emphasizing time savings and documentation accuracy
  • Mapped buyer journey from awareness through implementation
HubSpot CRM Implementation
  • Implemented complete HubSpot CRM architecture for rapid scaling
  • Built lead scoring system to prioritize high-intent prospects
  • Created routing workflows to match leads with appropriate sales resources
  • Set up qualification automation to accelerate sales cycles
Marketing Automation
  • Developed automated nurture campaigns for different buyer stages
  • Created email sequences targeting specific clinical specialties
  • Built demo request and trial signup workflows
  • Implemented lead enrichment with third-party data sources
Campaign Execution
  • Targeted campaigns reaching providers interested in AI documentation tools
  • Content strategy focused on clinical workflow improvements
  • Leveraged emerging AI technology enthusiasm in healthcare market

Key Learnings

Speed matters in emerging markets

AI healthcare is moving fast—having CRM infrastructure ready from day one enabled rapid scaling.

Healthcare buyers need education

AI medical scribes are new to many providers—content explaining workflow benefits drove conversions.

Lead scoring prioritizes resources

In a high-growth environment, automated lead scoring ensured sales focused on highest-intent prospects.

Technology enthusiasm creates opportunity

The current excitement around AI in healthcare creates receptive audiences for well-positioned solutions.
