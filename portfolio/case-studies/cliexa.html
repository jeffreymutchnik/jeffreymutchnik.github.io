The Challenge

cliexa is a digital health startup that developed patient engagement tools for chronic disease management, including solutions for rheumatoid arthritis, cardiovascular health, and other chronic conditions. When I joined as the first marketing hire, the company faced significant challenges:

  • No established marketing presence or brand awareness in digital health
  • Early-stage startup competing against established healthcare technology vendors
  • Complex B2B healthcare sales cycle requiring credibility and trust
  • Need to position innovative patient engagement technology to health systems
  • Limited budget requiring creative, high-impact marketing approaches

The Approach

I established comprehensive marketing infrastructure and brand presence from the ground up:

SEO & Content Strategy
  • Developed comprehensive SEO strategy targeting digital health and patient engagement keywords
  • Created educational content around chronic disease management and remote patient monitoring
  • Built thought leadership positioning cliexa as innovator in patient engagement
  • Achieved 826% increase in organic traffic through technical SEO and content optimization
Conversion Rate Optimization
  • Redesigned website with conversion-focused landing pages
  • Implemented lead capture mechanisms optimized for healthcare decision-makers
  • Created gated content assets (whitepapers, case studies, ROI calculators)
  • Achieved 4,350% increase in lead capture through systematic CRO
Strategic Partnerships
  • Secured partnership with American College of Cardiology (ACC)
  • Advanced to ACC Innovation Challenge finalist (top healthcare innovation competition)
  • Leveraged partnership for credibility and co-marketing opportunities
  • Built relationships with key opinion leaders in cardiology and rheumatology
Brand & Creative Direction
  • Established brand identity and visual language for B2B healthcare audience
  • Created sales collateral, product demos, and presentation materials
  • Developed conference presence for major healthcare events (HIMSS, ACC)
  • Built award-winning brand recognized by Denver startup community
Marketing Technology Stack
  • Built initial CRM and marketing automation infrastructure
  • Implemented analytics tracking for ROI measurement
  • Created lead scoring and qualification processes
  • Established foundation for scalable demand generation

Key Learnings

Credibility accelerates healthcare sales

The ACC partnership provided instant credibility that dramatically shortened sales cycles with health systems and provider organizations.

Content drives organic discovery

Educational content around chronic disease management and patient engagement attracted qualified healthcare buyers searching for solutions.

Startup constraints force creativity

Limited budget meant focusing on high-impact activities—SEO, strategic partnerships, and conversion optimization delivered outsized returns.

First hire means building everything

Being the first marketing hire required wearing multiple hats—brand, content, demand gen, events, and creative—building the foundation for all future marketing efforts.
