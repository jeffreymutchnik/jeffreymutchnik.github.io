The Challenge

The American Academy of Sleep Medicine (AASM) is the premier professional organization for sleep medicine, serving 13,000+ physicians and healthcare professionals. As Marketing Technology Manager, I faced several challenges:

  • Fragmented web presence across 8+ properties (aasm.org, sleepmeeting.org, sleepeducation.org, etc.)
  • Legacy CRM systems with disconnected member data
  • Underperforming continuing medical education (CME) webinar program
  • No unified e-commerce solution for educational resources and accreditation materials
  • Team of 3 specialists needing structured marketing operations processes

The Approach

I led a comprehensive digital transformation spanning CRM, web properties, e-commerce, and marketing automation:

Enterprise HubSpot Migration
  • Led complete CRM migration to HubSpot Marketing Hub with 100% data integrity
  • Designed lifecycle stages, custom properties, and reporting dashboards for membership engagement
  • Built 70+ automated workflows including lead nurturing, webinar registration, and member onboarding
  • Expanded to HubSpot Service Hub with AI-powered chatbot and ticketing workflows
Web Property Modernization
  • Rebuilt 8+ public web properties with consistent branding and improved user experience
  • Integrated HubSpot across all properties for unified data collection
  • Improved provider education resources and lead capture mechanisms
  • Cross-functional collaboration with IT, product, creative, and customer success teams
E-commerce Platform
  • Built Shopify e-commerce platform for clinical practice resources and accreditation materials
  • Implemented payment processing and inventory management
  • Integrated with HubSpot CRM for unified customer view
  • Generated $500K+ in annual sales
Webinar & Event Marketing
  • Transformed CME webinar program with automated nurture campaigns
  • Multi-brand email marketing optimization across different AASM properties
  • Achieved record-breaking attendance for SLEEP annual conference (largest sleep medicine event in North America)
